# Code of Conduct

The Halo project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct.

## In short

We expect contributors and maintainers to engage with respect, good faith, and professional courtesy. Disagreement on technical matters is welcome; personal attacks, harassment, or sustained disruption are not.

## Reporting

Concerns can be sent privately to **sebastien.denes@sap.com**. Reports are kept confidential. Maintainers will respond within seven days and may take any action they consider proportionate, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the project's community spaces.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, the project's npm pages, and any official off-platform channels — and to anyone representing Halo in public.
